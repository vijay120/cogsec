# Weekly Review Template

Do this every Sunday. 15-20 minutes. This is where the compound interest happens.

---

## The Point of This Review

This review replaces the "review your journal" step you never did. Instead of re-reading entries, you extract patterns and convert them into next week's actions. The entries themselves are disposable. The patterns are the value.
