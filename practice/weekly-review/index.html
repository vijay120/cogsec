    Weekly Review Template¶

    Do this every Sunday. 15-20 minutes. This is where the compound interest happens.

    Week of: ___¶

    1. Score Review¶

    How many days this week scored 8+ on the daily checklist? ___/7

    Trend vs. last week: Better / Same / Worse

    2. Foundation Check¶

    Foundation This week Notes
    Sleep (7-9 hrs, consistent?) ___/7 nights
    Exercise (30 min, moderate?) ___/5 sessions
    Meditation (10 min?) ___/7 days
    Morning no-phone window? ___/7 days

    If any foundation < 50% compliance: That's this week's priority. Don't add new practices until foundations are solid.

    3. CBT Pattern Review¶

    Scan your CBT journal entries from this week:

    • Most common distortion: ___
    • Most common trigger: ___
    • Did balanced thoughts feel genuine? ___
    • Any recurring automatic thought showing up? ___

    Action: Write one implementation intention targeting the most common trigger:

    "If __, then I will __"

    4. Deep Work Audit¶

    • How many 60-90 min focus blocks this week? ___
    • Did you use the post-block reward protocol? ___
    • Any browsing/social media relapses after focus blocks? ___
    • Music during deep work: silence/ambient or lyrics? ___

    5. Social Environment¶

    • Meaningful conversations this week (intellectual + emotional depth): ___
    • Any new community attempts? ___
    • Maintained a remote connection? ___
    • Hosted or initiated anything? ___

    6. Gratitude Audit¶

    • Did evening gratitudes most days? ___/7
    • Were they specific (not generic)? ___
    • One gratitude that stands out from this week:

    7. Pre-Mortem (if applicable)¶

    Any major decision or project coming up next week?

    If yes:

    • Assume it failed. Why? ___
    • Preventive action: ___

    8. Values Alignment Check (monthly — do on first Sunday of month)¶

    • Are my current goals still aligned with my values? ___
    • Am I spending time on things that matter to me? ___
    • Anything I'm doing out of obligation that I should drop or renegotiate? ___

    9. Next Week's Focus¶

    One thing to improve: ___

    One thing to maintain: ___

    Updated implementation intentions: 1. If __, then I will __ 2. If __, then I will __ 3. If __, then I will __

    The Point of This Review¶

    This review replaces the "review your journal" step you never did. Instead of re-reading entries, you extract patterns and convert them into next week's actions. The entries themselves are disposable. The patterns are the value.
