    Daily Checklist¶

    Print this or copy into your tracking tool. Check off as you go.

    Morning Block (15 min total)¶

    • No phone for first 30 min after waking

    • 3 intentions written (before opening email/Slack/anything)

    • _____ because ___
    • _____ because ___
    • _____ because ___

    • Exercise (30 min, or note what you'll do later today)

    • Meditation (10 min — sit, breath, notice, return)

    During the Day¶

    Deep Work Blocks¶

    • Pre-work: Music ritual (1-2 songs), then silence/ambient for the block

    • Phone in another room during focus blocks

    • Block length: 60-90 min, then mandatory break

    • Post-block reward: Stand up + walk (NOT browsing). Write 3 sentences about what you accomplished.

    Metacognitive Check-ins (no time cost — just notice)¶

    • Caught a thought pattern? Label it: planning / judging / worrying / craving / other

    • Emotional flag fired? Named the emotion before reacting? (even once counts)

    • Implementation intention held? Did your "If X, then Y" fire today?

    If a Difficult Moment Happened¶

    • CBT reappraisal (5 min — use practice/cbt-journal.md template)
    • Situation: ___
    • Automatic thought: ___
    • Distortion type: ___
    • Balanced thought: ___

    Evening Block (5 min total)¶

    • 3 specific gratitudes (what happened + why it mattered)
    • 3-line review:
    • What worked today? ___
    • What didn't? ___
    • What will I do differently tomorrow? ___

    • Tomorrow's top priority identified (just one)

    • Phone in another room by 10pm

    • Screens off 30 min before target sleep time

    Scoring (optional, for tracker)¶

    Count your checks. Don't aim for 100% — aim for consistency.

    Score Meaning
    12+ Excellent day — full practice
    8-11 Good day — core practices hit
    4-7 Partial day — foundations covered
    0-3 Reset day — no judgment, try again tomorrow

    The goal is not perfection. The goal is that most days land in the 8+ range over time.
